

1. No permissions
Privacy Friendly Ludo does not require any permissions.

2. No advertisement
Moreover, Privacy Friendly Ludo distinguishes from many other applications in the way that it completely relinquishes advertisements. Advertisement might track a user's actions. It could also shorten battery life or use mobile data.

Privacy Friendly Ludo belongs to the Privacy Friendly Apps group developed by the research group SECUSO at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology. More information can be found at https://secuso.org/pfa
